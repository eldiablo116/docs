## Navigating the Documentation

All services and guides are organized in the left-hand navigation menu. Each section contains detailed guides, step-by-step instructions, and troubleshooting information to help you get the most out of ARCEUS.

### Welcome

This section provides an introduction to ARCEUS and helps you understand how to navigate the documentation. Start here if you're new to ARCEUS or need an overview of what's available.

### ARCEUS 101

Essential information for getting started with ARCEUS. This section covers the basics, including how to get help when you need it. Whether you're troubleshooting a problem or looking for support, you'll find the information you need here.

### Plex

Complete guides for setting up and using Plex with ARCEUS. Learn how to accept your invite, install the Plex app on your devices, configure playback settings for optimal streaming, make content requests, and report issues with your media library.

### Jellyfin

Guides for connecting to and using Jellyfin with ARCEUS. This section includes instructions for connecting to your Jellyfin server, recommended apps for different platforms, making content requests, and reporting media-related issues.

### TV

Everything you need to know about live TV streaming with ARCEUS. Find information about VPN setup, installing TV apps on Android devices, optimizing TiviMate settings, and recommended apps for iOS, iPadOS, and tvOS devices.

### Resources

Access community resources and essential tools. Connect with other users on Discord, access the ARCEUS portal, and find additional support channels to enhance your ARCEUS experience.
